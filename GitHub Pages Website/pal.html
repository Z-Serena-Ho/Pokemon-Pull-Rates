This is the second set of the Scarlet & Violet Era for the Pokemon Trading Card Game. It features all three Starter Pokemon (Sprigatito, Fuecoco, and Quaxly) as well as their evolutions. There's also the Treasures of Ruin (Wo-Chien, Chien-Pao, Ting-Lu, and Chi-Yu) and many more! Finally, the most popular Trainer included is Iono.
